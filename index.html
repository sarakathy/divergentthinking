Your Creations

When you want to be creative, just remember it's already inside you. Just keep in mind these four things you practiced today.

1
Go for quantity.
Come up with lots of ideas! You made the same shape into many different things!
2
Build on ideas.
You used your ideas to spark other ideas. One thought leads to the next!
3
Seek unusual ideas.
You tried to think of something others wouldn't! Unusual ideas are often the most powerful.
4
Wait to judge ideas.
You drew even the ideas your inner critic doubted. Evaluating comes later; let your ideas flow.

Whenever you face a problem or challenge that needs new thinking, know that you are creative!
